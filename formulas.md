## Permutations:

$$
_{n} P_{r}=\frac{n !}{(n-r) !}
$$

## Combinations:

$$
_n C_r=\frac{n !}{r ! (n-r) !}
$$

## Factorial: 
$$
n!=n×(n−1)×(n−2)×…×2×1
$$

## Binomial Coefficient:
The binomial coefficient is the number of ways of picking unordered outcomes from possibilities
$$
\binom {n}{k}=\frac {n!}{k!(n-k)!}
$$
